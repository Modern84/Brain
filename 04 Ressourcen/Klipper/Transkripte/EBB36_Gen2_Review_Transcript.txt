⚠️ Correction (Wiring Clarification):

In this video I incorrectly stated that the USB adapter board is not required when running the EBB36 Gen 2 in CAN mode. In the configuration shown here, the USB adapter board is required for proper power delivery and safe operation. Please ensure it is installed as intended.

Additionally, you do not need to cut the MX3.0 connector off the included cable.

The correct wiring is as follows:
- Create a new 2-pin CAN cable from your main control board (for example, an Octopus Max EZ).
- Connect that cable to the 2-pin CAN header on the USB adapter board.
- Use the provided MX3.0 to XT30 cable to connect from the USB adapter board to the EBB36 Gen 2.

Do not modify the included MX3.0 cable. Use it as supplied between the USB adapter and the EBB36 Gen 2.


In this video, I take a deep dive into the BigTreeTech EBB36 Gen 2 toolhead board and compare it directly to the Gen 1 version.

On screen, you'll see the EBB36 Gen 1 on the left and the Gen 2 — along with the included USB adapter board — on the right, so you'll always know exactly which hardware I'm referencing.

This is a fully independent review. I purchased all of the hardware shown in this video myself at full retail price. No sponsorships. No influence. Just real-world evaluation.

We'll cover:

What's new in Gen 2
- USB vs CAN communication differences
- Built-in USB adapter vs external U2C requirement (Gen 1)
- Jumper-based mode switching
- Practical implications for Klipper builds
- Hardware layout changes
- Whether upgrading from Gen 1 makes sense

One of the biggest improvements with Gen 2 is flexibility. Instead of being CAN-only like Gen 1, Gen 2 includes both the toolhead board and USB adapter in one package, allowing you to switch between USB and CAN with a simple jumper change. That simplifies wiring, lowers the barrier to entry, and gives you options depending on your printer setup.

If you're building or upgrading a Klipper-based 3D printer and considering the EBB36 platform, this breakdown will help you decide which version fits your needs.
